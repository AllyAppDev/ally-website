Acceptable Use Policy

Last updated: July 2026 ~7 min read

This policy defines what you can and cannot do on Ally. We believe in free expression and resist overreach — but some things are simply not acceptable anywhere. This document is about those bright lines.

1 Purpose

Ally's Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") exists to protect our users and keep Ally a safe, functional platform. It applies to everyone — individual users, bot developers, and server administrators.

This AUP is incorporated into our Terms of Service by reference. Violating it means violating your agreement with Ally.

We believe in a minimal rule set with clear consequences, not an exhaustive list designed to catch people on technicalities. If something is illegal, genuinely harmful to others, or would make Ally unsafe for our community, it's prohibited here.

2 Prohibited Content

The following content is strictly prohibited on Ally and will result in immediate action — including permanent account termination and, where required by law, reports to authorities:

🚫

Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM)

Any sexual content involving minors is absolutely prohibited and will be reported to NCMEC and relevant law enforcement. This is a zero-tolerance policy with no appeals.

🚫

Terrorism & Violent Extremism

Content that promotes, glorifies, or facilitates acts of terrorism, mass violence, or genocide. This includes recruitment materials, incitement to violence, and content from designated terrorist organizations.

🚫

Doxxing & Non-Consensual Sharing

Publishing another person's private information (home address, workplace, phone number, personal photos, financial information) without their consent — especially with intent to harass or harm.

🚫

Harassment & Threats

Content designed to harass, intimidate, or threaten specific individuals. This includes coordinated harassment campaigns, sustained targeted abuse, and credible threats of violence.

🚫

Non-Consensual Intimate Imagery

Distributing sexual images or recordings of someone without their consent ("revenge porn"). We treat all reports of this with urgency.

⚠️

Spam & Unsolicited Bulk Messaging

Sending mass unsolicited messages, coordinated inauthentic behavior, automated spam, or manipulating platform systems to artificially inflate engagement or spread content.

⚠️

Malware & Malicious Code

Distributing viruses, ransomware, phishing links, credential stealers, or any software designed to damage systems or steal data. This includes linking to such content from Ally.

⚠️

Illegal Content

Content that is illegal in the jurisdiction where it's hosted or where it causes harm — including instructions for creating weapons capable of mass harm, trafficking-related content, and fraudulent schemes.

3 Prohibited Behaviors

Beyond content, these actions are also prohibited:

🚫

Ban Evasion

Creating new accounts to circumvent a platform ban — or a server ban where the server admin has banned you. If you believe a ban was unjust, use the appeals process.

🚫

Coordinated Raiding

Organizing or participating in coordinated attacks on communities — spamming a server, bombarding members with unwanted content, or attempting to disrupt or destroy a community.

🚫

Impersonation

Creating accounts designed to deceive others into believing you are a specific real person, organization, or Ally staff member. Parody accounts are permitted if clearly labeled.

🚫

Platform Exploitation

Attempting to exploit bugs or vulnerabilities, scraping Ally without authorization, reverse-engineering the app to circumvent security features, or using Ally's infrastructure for unauthorized purposes.

⚠️

Undermining Platform Integrity

Actions that degrade service quality for others — including operating botnets, running DDoS attacks against Ally or other users, or manipulating platform algorithms through inauthentic means.

4 Enforcement

We enforce this policy fairly, proportionally, and consistently. Our enforcement framework:

⚠️

Warning

For first-time or minor violations. You'll receive a clear explanation of what rule was broken.

⏸️

Timeout / Suspension

Temporary restriction of posting, DMs, or account access. Duration depends on severity and history.

🔴

Permanent Ban

For severe violations or a pattern of serious misconduct. Applied immediately for zero-tolerance violations.

Enforcement is applied by our Trust & Safety team. We reserve the right to skip warnings and proceed directly to suspension or permanent ban for serious violations (CSAM, active security attacks, terrorism content).

5 Appeals Process

📬 Every enforcement action — except zero-tolerance violations — is appealable.

If you believe we made a mistake, here's how to appeal:

  1. Email appeals@allyapp.cc within 30 days of the enforcement action.
  2. Include your username or account ID, a description of the action taken, and your reasoning for why you believe it was incorrect.
  3. A different Trust & Safety team member (not the one who made the original decision) will review your appeal within 14 days.
  4. We'll respond with our decision and the reasoning behind it.

We take appeals seriously. If we made an error, we'll reverse the decision and apologize. Our goal is to get it right, not to just close tickets.

6 Reporting Violations

Reporting is the most important tool our community has. If you see something that violates this policy:

  • Use the Report button on any message, server, or profile — it's available in every context menu.
  • For urgent safety issues (active threats, CSAM), email safety@allyapp.cc directly. We monitor this 24/7.
  • For CSAM specifically, you can also report directly to NCMEC at CyberTipline.org.

Reports are confidential — we don't reveal the identity of reporters. We take all reports seriously and respond within 24–72 hours for most cases, faster for urgent safety matters.

✅ Good-faith reports are always welcome, even if no violation is found. False reporting designed to harm others is itself a violation of this policy.
