Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026 ~10 min read

Privacy isn't a checkbox for us — it's the core of why Ally exists. This document explains exactly what we do (and don't do) with your data.

1 Our Commitment

Ally was built because too many platforms treat user data as a product. We take the opposite approach: your data is yours, and our job is to protect it.

🚫

No data sales — ever

We will never sell your personal data to third parties. Full stop.

🔒

No behavioral ads

Ally has zero advertising. We don't profile you, track you, or monetize your behavior.

🤖

No AI training on your content

Your messages and files are never used to train AI or machine learning models.

🛡️

Encryption by default

All data is encrypted in transit. E2EE for DMs is implemented and active.

2 What We Collect

We collect only what we need to make Ally work. Here's a clear breakdown:

Category What specifically Why we need it
Account data Username, email address, hashed password, profile photo (optional) To create and manage your account
Authentication Login timestamps, IP addresses at login, device identifiers Security — to detect unauthorized access and support 2FA
Message metadata Timestamps, sender/recipient IDs, server/channel IDs, message IDs To deliver messages and support features like read receipts
Message content Text, images, files, reactions you post To transmit and store your communications
Usage signals Feature usage (e.g., "used voice channel"), error logs To fix bugs and improve Ally — never tied to ad targeting
Payment data Billing country, subscription status (payment card details held by processor, not us) To process premium subscriptions

Uploaded files

When you upload images, we automatically strip EXIF metadata (GPS coordinates, device info, timestamps) before storing the file. This happens server-side and cannot be disabled — we believe it's the right default.

3 What We Don't Collect

❌ No tracking pixels or third-party analytics scripts on Ally services

❌ No cross-site tracking or browsing history outside of Ally

❌ No biometric data (face scans, fingerprints, voice identification)

❌ No location data beyond what's implicit in an IP address at login

❌ No data purchased from or shared with data brokers

We do use our own basic server-side analytics (page views, API request counts, error rates) to run the platform. These are aggregate metrics, never linked to individual profiles, and never shared externally.

4 Your Rights

You have meaningful control over your data. Here's what you can do — and how:

Export your data

You can request a full export of your data (account info, messages, uploaded files) from Account Settings → Privacy → Request Data Export. We'll prepare a machine-readable archive and notify you when it's ready, within 30 days.

Delete your account

You can delete your account at any time from Account Settings → Danger Zone. We'll delete your active data within 30 days and purge all backup copies within 90 days.

Correct your information

You can update most of your account information (username, email, profile) directly from your settings. For corrections we can't automate, email privacy@allyapp.cc.

Opt out of non-essential data uses

From your Privacy Settings, you can opt out of crash reporting and usage analytics. This doesn't affect core functionality.

GDPR, CCPA, and other regulations

If you're in the EU, UK, or California, you have additional rights (access, rectification, erasure, portability, objection). To exercise them, contact privacy@allyapp.cc. We respond within 30 days.

5 Data Retention

🗓️ Active data deleted within 30 days of account deletion. Backups fully purged within 90 days.

While your account is active, we retain your data to provide the service. The specific retention periods:

  • Messages & content: Retained until you delete them or your account. Deleted content is removed from active databases within 30 days.
  • Account data: Retained until account deletion, then purged within 30 days.
  • Security logs (IP addresses, login history): Retained for 90 days to support abuse investigations, then deleted.
  • Billing records: Retained for 7 years as required by law, but linked only to a transaction ID — not to your messages or content.
  • Backup systems: Backups are rotated and any data from deleted accounts is fully purged within 90 days of deletion.

6 Third Parties

We use a small number of trusted service providers to operate Ally. They're listed below. We do not share your data with data brokers, advertising networks, or analytics platforms.

Provider Purpose What they access
Cloudflare CDN, DDoS protection, DNS IP addresses, request metadata (no message content)
Payment processor (Stripe) Premium subscription billing Payment card details, billing address — never your messages
Email provider (transactional) Account emails, password resets, notifications Your email address and notification content you've opted into
AI moderation service (optional) Scanning for CSAM and severe policy violations in public spaces Public channel content only — never private DMs

Every provider we use is contractually bound to use your data only for the specific purpose above and never for their own advertising or analytics.

7 Children's Privacy

Ally is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction).

If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under the minimum age, we will delete it promptly. If you believe we've inadvertently collected data from a child, please contact privacy@allyapp.cc immediately.

8 Security

We take security seriously and implement industry-standard measures:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.2+.
  • Databases are encrypted at rest.
  • End-to-end encryption is implemented for DMs using the Signal Protocol.
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt — we cannot recover your plaintext password.
  • We conduct regular security reviews and penetration tests.
  • We operate a responsible disclosure program at security@allyapp.cc.

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you within 72 hours via email and in-app notification.

9 Changes to This Policy

We'll update this policy as Ally grows and laws change. When we make material changes (anything that meaningfully affects how we use your data), we'll notify you via email and in-app with at least 14 days' notice before the changes take effect.

The "Last updated" date at the top of this page always reflects the most recent version.

10 Contact & Data Requests

For all privacy questions, data requests, or concerns:

  • Privacy requests (access, deletion, export): privacy@allyapp.cc
  • Security vulnerabilities: security@allyapp.cc
  • General inquiries: hello@allyapp.cc

We aim to respond to all privacy requests within 7 business days and complete them within 30 days.
